The EOs

  • Executive Order 14146 Declaring a National Energy Emergency
    • Convening of the God Squad Quarterly
  • Executive Order 14225 Immediate Expansion of American Timber Production
    • Can use emergency consultation
  • Secretarial Memo 1078-006 Increasing Timber Production and Designating an Emergency Situation on National Forest Sytem Lands
    • if half of project is within this designated area
    • Should use Emergency Consultation to comply with ESA
  • Associate Chief's Memo: Implementation of Secretarial Memo 1078-006

Some Random Notes

  • You can use emergency consultation for timber and energy, with the exception of wind and solar.
  • EOs have "thou shalt".
  • What is an emergency: typically those are not predictable events. Timber sales under the current EOs are "Emergencies".
  • Emergency consultations are not exemptions from ESA.

The Gist

The most important part of this training was that line officers need to decide if they want to use emergency consultation or not, but that the administration and managers will be monitoring how many Emergency Consultations that we use.

Also, that emergency consultations are not exemptions from consultation and that the consultations will need to be done at some point.
